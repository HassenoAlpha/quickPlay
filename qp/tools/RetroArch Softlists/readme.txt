RetroArch Softlists for QuickPlay
This will move the RetroArch softlists into your Data directory
It will be visible in QuickPlay's ROMs sidebar once you hit F5 there
You need to do an efind for Retroarch Mame Emulators for it to work
see here for in depth instructions:
